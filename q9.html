How long does processing and shipping take?

The time it takes for you to receive your order will depend on your location, the item(s) ordered, and the shipping speed you selected. All shipments take place in two stages: processing and transit. Processing Processing and packing times are estimated 24-48 hours. This excludes orders placed on the weekend. Transit Economy delivery service 4-8 business days Standard delivery service 2-6 business days Express delivery service 2-4 business days Overnight delivery service 1-3 business days Time frames are dependent upon order date and your location.
